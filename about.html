About Me

From the sunny shores of the Bahamas to chilly Engineering Labs in Calgary, I am not your stereotypical Engineer.

My culinary story began at age 9 in my mother's kitchen in the Bahamas. These moments weren't just about baking; they were lessons in passion, perseverance, and the joy of creating something people love.

Today, I balance my engineering studies with my love for the kitchen. This space is where I share recipes that blend my Bahamian heritage with new discoveries, proving that you don't have to choose between technical precision and creative passion.
